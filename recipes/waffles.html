Simple Waffle Recipe

This is the easiest waffle recipe you will ever come across. Enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • Box of forzen Eggo waffles
  • Butter (optional)
  • Syrup of your choice

Steps:

  1. Put desired amount of forzen waffles into your toaster (toast to your liking)
  2. Once toasted, put waffles on plate (careful, they'll be hot!)
  3. If desired, add butter to your waffles
  4. Pour desired amount of syrup over waffles
