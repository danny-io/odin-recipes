Delicious Street Tacos Recipe

Tacos are one of the most delicious foods around. Contrary to popular belief, we don't only have to have them on Tuesdays. Below is an amazing authentic Mexican Street Taco recipe. Enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of chopped carne asada
  • 1 pack of street taco sized corn tortillas
  • Vegtable oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Lemon
  • Salsa of choice

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat a pan and use 1 tbs of vegtable oil on medium heat
  2. Put carne asada in pan while stiring
  3. Season with salt and pepper to your liking
  4. If you are using Onion, Cilantro, and Lemon, chop to your liking
  5. Over griddle, or using the micrwave, heat the tortillas
