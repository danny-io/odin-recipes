Easy Cereal Recipe

This is the easiest cereal recipe you'll ever come across. Enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • Boxed cereal of your choice
  • Milk of your choice
  • Fruit topping(s) (optional)
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Pour cereal into your favorite bowl
  2. Add your favorite fruit toppings (optional)
  3. Pour milk over cereal and fruit toppings
